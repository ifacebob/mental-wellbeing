How It Helps Students

Mental Health Support

University students often face significant stress and mental health challenges. Our Mood Journal helps them:

  • Track emotional patterns over time
  • Identify triggers for stress and anxiety
  • Develop self-awareness and reflection habits
  • Access AI-powered insights about their mental state

Academic Support

The Study Buddy feature assists with learning by:

  • Transforming notes into interactive study materials
  • Using active recall for better information retention
  • Making studying more engaging and efficient
  • Reducing study stress through better preparation
